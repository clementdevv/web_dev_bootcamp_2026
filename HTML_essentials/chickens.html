Chicken

The chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus) is a domesticated subspecies of the red junglefowl (Gallus gallus), originally native to Southeast Asia.

It was first domesticated around 8,000 years ago and is one of the most common and widespread domesticated animals in the world.

Diet

Chickens are omnivores. In the wild, they scratch at the soil to search for:

  • Seeds
  • Insects
  • Lizards
  • Small snakes
  • Young mice
